About Daries' Sweets & Desserts


Homemade since 2017

Daries' Sweets & Desserts consists three beautiful women that are managed this business together in 2017 as our part time since we're still study at UiTM Machang at that time in business course. At first, we just focus on the students of UiTM Machang only by providing two main menu which are Banana Cake and Brownies Cookies, but our business are growth very well. So, we decided to add some menu such as Royal Pudding and Cake Batik. All of our menu are special homemadeby three of us since we want to maintain our quality for our lovely customers.

However, after we graduated the three of us decided to further expand our business market. We have opened a store called Daries Sweets & Dessert. All ingredients that we are used are halal, preservative-free and high quality ingredients.
